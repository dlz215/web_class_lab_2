Some of my favorite things

Favorite TV Series

My favorite TV series is Twin Peaks, a mystery/horror series created by David Lynch and Mark Frost. The original run consisted of two seasons that aired in 1990-1991. A 3rd season was aired in 2017. The series is known for mixing elements of the detective and soap opera genres with the dark, surreal, and complex storytelling methods of its directors.

Official Twin Peaks website

Favorite Music

My favorite musical act is Deathspell Omega, a French black metal band that formed in 1998 and is currently active. After starting with a traditional black metal style influenced by the Scandinavian scene of the early 90s, they gained more notoriety and acclaim after taking their sound in a more experimental direction.
